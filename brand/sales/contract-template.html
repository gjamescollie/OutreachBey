This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the date above between Lucayan Labs ("Provider") and the Client named above. The parties agree to the following terms:

Services

Provider will deploy and operate Cay Receptionist — an AI employee service that handles inbound WhatsApp inquiries on the Client's designated WhatsApp number. Services include:

  • AI configuration customized for the Client's business, tone, and customer base
  • Knowledge base built from the Client's provided FAQs and business details
  • 24/7 automated inbound inquiry handling, lead capture, and booking qualification
  • Escalation routing to the Client's designated Cay Control channel
  • Access to a private operator dashboard for logs, contacts, and analytics
  • Ongoing support and knowledge base maintenance as included in the monthly fee

Services do not include live calendar availability, payment processing, outbound messaging campaigns, or multi-number deployments unless separately agreed in writing.

Term

This Agreement begins on the go-live date and continues on a month-to-month basis. Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice. Provider may terminate immediately for non-payment or breach of this Agreement.

Fees and Payment

Setup fee: $2,500 USD, due before onboarding begins. Non-refundable once onboarding work has commenced.

Monthly retainer: $499 USD per month, billed on the go-live date each month. Payment is due within 7 days of invoice. Late payments (beyond 14 days) may result in service suspension.

Payment methods accepted: bank transfer, credit card. All amounts are in USD. Client is responsible for any applicable taxes in their jurisdiction.

Client Obligations

The Client agrees to:

  • Provide accurate business information, FAQs, and pricing for knowledge base setup
  • Provide access to the designated WhatsApp number for linking (QR scan during onboarding)
  • Designate a Cay Control contact number for escalation notifications
  • Review and approve the knowledge base before go-live
  • Promptly follow up on escalated leads and complaints as notified
  • Not use the service to send spam, unsolicited messages, or content that violates WhatsApp's Terms of Service

Intellectual Property

The Cay AI software, AI models, and Lucayan Labs technology remain the exclusive property of Provider. Client data — including contacts, conversation logs, and business information — remains the property of the Client. Provider will not share or sell Client data to third parties.

Confidentiality

Each party agrees to keep the other's confidential information private and not to disclose it to third parties without written consent. This includes business data, pricing, customer details, and technical implementation details. This obligation survives termination of the Agreement.

Limitation of Liability

Provider's total liability under this Agreement is limited to the fees paid by the Client in the three months preceding the claim. Provider is not liable for: indirect, incidental, or consequential damages; lost revenue, profits, or bookings; WhatsApp service interruptions or policy changes by Meta; or inaccurate AI responses arising from inaccurate information provided by the Client.

WhatsApp Compliance

Client acknowledges that use of WhatsApp via a linked device operates under WhatsApp's Terms of Service. Provider will operate the service in a manner designed to minimize risk, but Client accepts responsibility for their WhatsApp number and any consequences of use. Provider is not affiliated with Meta or WhatsApp.

Termination and Data

Upon termination, Provider will provide the Client with a final export of their contacts and conversation logs in CSV format within 7 business days. After that period, Provider may delete Client data from its systems. The setup fee is non-refundable. Monthly fees are not refundable for partial months.
