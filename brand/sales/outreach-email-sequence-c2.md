## Notes

- Never send a fourth email. If three touches get no reply, move to a phone call or mark `Closed — not now` and revisit in 60 days.
- Personalize the bracketed fields for every prospect — generic emails get ignored.
- Subject lines that reference the prospect's business name by name outperform generic ones.
- Sending from your personal email (gjamescollie@gmail.com) outperforms a branded domain for cold outreach at this stage — it looks like a person, not a campaign.
